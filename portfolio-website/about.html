About the Creator

I began a career in graphic design in 2014, I began as a freelance artist while attending Wichita State University. Upon graduation, I began a career as an animator at the non-profit "Teach for Life". While working with the talented people at Teach for Life I continued doing freelance graphic design work and also began to take an interest in digital media. I now live in beautiful Boulder Colorado and I am currently inthrawed in the wonderful world of web development.
